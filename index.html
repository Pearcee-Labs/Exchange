Welcome to our Comenius project on the Möbius strip! Over the course of 2011-2013, the schools Devonport High School for Girls in the UK, XV.Gimnazija in Croatia, Justus-von-Liebig Gymnasium in Germany and Colegiul Naţional "Moise Nicoară" in Romania have been conducting projects that revolve around exploring the ins and outs of the ‘magic’ properties of the Möbius Strip.
