The Möbius Strip in Life

“A devilishly twisted device that can tangle your mind and lead you to peculiar places”

Does Möbius just apply to Mathematics?

There are many preconceptions that the Möbius strip is only put into practice in mathematics, but really it applies to life. Our rollercoaster ride of inevitabilities and twists and turns is a lot like a Möbius strip. How we deal with events in our lives and the question of cause and effect is also similar to the wondrous mathematical shape, which represents how one thing leads into another; in fact, this fluency and continuity of life and how one event influences and leads into many others is rather unlike maths!

What can the Möbius strip represent in our lives?

The Möbius strip can act as a visual representation of the many different events in our lives and how things happen for a reason. The Möbius strip can symbolise how something causes something else and will always have an effect on it. This continuation of life events over time is shown through the twist in the Möbius strip.

How is the Möbius strip applied in real life?

The Möbius strip has many applications throughout the physical and mathematical world. Within the physical world the Möbius strip represents continuity and eternity; it has many applications in the use of jewellery and other such items to represent longevity. With its one plane and continuous face it is a mathematical phenomenon; which has been transferred into many uses in engineering, such as the conveyor belt. The belt is continuous and with the twist in it, the belt gets less worn down because it has a higher surface area than it would have without the twist and therefore Möbius strip shape to ensure the rubber will last longer as the entire surface of the rubber will be used on the conveyor belt, instead of just one side.
